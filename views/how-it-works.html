How it Works


PevPot is the only Positive Expected Value lottery. Here's how it works:


1. Start of Draw

A new draw starts every thousand bitcoin blocks (aprox. 1 week). When it starts the pot is empty.

2. Players buy Tickets

Players buy tickets using bitcoin. For every (non-dust) satoshi sent you gets 1 ticket to win.

The size of the pot is determined by the sum of all player's tickets. We don't know for sure until the end.

So that means, the more tickets sold the less chance of winning but the bigger the prize

3. Draw Ends

The draw will finish after a thousand bitcoin blocks.

The size of the pot gets locked and it will become the prize for the winner.

4. The Winner is Picked

We pick a random ticket to win after the draw is over (this takes a few hours, to avoid the possibility of miners cheating). For more information of how this process is done check out our Provably fair page.

The lucky winner is announced, which can be seen on the draw page and within 24 hours or so will be sent their prize!

5. Early Bird Bonus

In addition to winning the entire pot, the winner also qualifies for a percent of early bird bonus, which is graciously filled by the draw sponsors. The earlier in the draw that you bought your tickets, the more of the early bird bonuses you get!

This is what makes the lottery Positive Expected Value because players get out more than what they do, and is designed to encourage players to not wait until the last moment to buy tickets.

6. New Draw

Meanwhile, a new draw has started. Right after a draw ends, another starts with the next thousand bitcoin blocks.

The pot is empty again until more tickets are bought, and so on.

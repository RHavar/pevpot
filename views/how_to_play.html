How to Play


Playing direct

This can only be done from a bitcoin wallet in which supports both receiving and signing a message from the sending address.

Send the money to the lotto address

From your bitcoin wallet send the money to {{ lottoAddress }}. Every satoshi sent gives you one chance of winning. The minimium send is 0.0001 BTC (100 bits) and there is no maximium.

Register (Optional)

Register your sending bitcoin address (the first of the inputs) by signing a message. This step is not mandatory at this point.

Win (hopefully!)

Our provably fair algorithm will pick the winner, which will be shown on the page. If you won, and have not yet done so you will need to register your sending address in the next three months. The entire pot will be sent to registered address!

Play from anywhere

This is easy and our recommended method, because you do not need to register after creating your forwarding address.

Create a forwarding address.

Create a forwarding address by giving us the address you'd like your winnings to go to. Make sure to save (and verify) our letter of guarantee in case anything goes wrong.

Send money to the forwarding address.

All money sent to your forwarding address, will automatically be forwarded to the lotto address (minus only that to cover mining fee costs).

Win (hopefully!)

Our provably fair algorithm will pick the winner of the entire pot, which will be announced on the page. If this is you, just sit back and relax and the pot will shortly be automatically be sent to your chosen address!
